About

I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate at the mechanical engineering department of UC Berkeley.

Prior to joining Berkeley, I obtained B.S. degrees in computer engineering and mechanical engineering from Georgia Institute of Technology, where I researched with Professors Julien Meaud and Thomas Conte.

I have interned at Microsoft, Scale AI, Honda Aircraft Company, and Tesla, Inc.

Research interests

My interests include (convex) optimization, deep learning, and controls.

I work on ensuring the adversarial robustness of neural networks and accelerating their training via theoretically disciplined methods, while still maintaining their impressive performances.
